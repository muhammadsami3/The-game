Manual To The Game

  • Turn over any two cards.

  • If the two cards match, the gamer win a point.

  • If they don't match, they are turn back over.

  • Remember what was on each card and where it was.

  • The game is over and the gamer win, when all the cards have been matched correctly.

  • The gamer lose if the time set is over or the life is over.

  • You lose life when you pick 2 unmatched cards sequentially.


Game Awards

  • Awaeded badge when getting highest score.

  • Awaeded badge when getting the game without any mistake.

  • Awarded badge for time bonus when getting the game at less than the half time.
